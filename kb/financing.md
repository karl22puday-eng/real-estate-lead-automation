# Crestview Realty — Financing & Fees

A plain-English guide to paying for a home and what working with us costs.

## Pre-qualification vs. pre-approval
- **Pre-qualification** is a quick estimate based on info you tell a lender. It's a
  rough guide, not a commitment.
- **Pre-approval** means the lender has verified your income, assets, and credit and will
  lend up to a stated amount. This is what you want — it sets your real budget and makes
  your offers credible. **Get pre-approved before you shop seriously.**

## How much do I need for a down payment?
- Many conventional loans allow **3–5% down**.
- **20% down** lets you avoid private mortgage insurance (PMI) and lowers your payment.
- **FHA** loans allow lower down payments for qualifying buyers; **VA** loans can offer
  0% down for eligible veterans/service members.
- The right answer depends on your finances and goals — a lender will lay out the options.

## Closing costs
Plan for roughly **2–5% of the purchase price** in closing costs (lender fees, title
insurance, escrow, appraisal, prepaid taxes and insurance). Some of these are
negotiable, and in some markets sellers contribute toward buyer closing costs.

## Cash buyers
Cash purchases skip the loan and appraisal steps, close faster (often 1–2 weeks), and are
very attractive to sellers. You'll need to show **proof of funds** with your offer.

## What does working with Crestview cost?
- **Buyers:** in most transactions, the buyer's agent commission is paid through the deal
  at closing, so representation is typically **free to the buyer**. We always confirm the
  exact arrangement for your specific purchase in writing before you make an offer.
- **Sellers:** listing commission is **negotiable** and agreed in writing before we list.
  We'll show you exactly what's included (photography, marketing, MLS, negotiation,
  transaction management) and how buyer-agent compensation is handled.

## Investors — the quick numbers we run
- **Cap rate** = net operating income ÷ purchase price.
- **Cash-on-cash** = annual pre-tax cash flow ÷ cash invested.
- **Rent estimate** vs. mortgage + taxes + insurance + maintenance to gauge cash flow.
We'll run a basic analysis on any property you're considering and connect you with a
property manager if you want it hands-off.

## The bottom line on budget
Your **pre-approval amount** and your **down payment** together set your real shopping
range. Sorting these out early is the single biggest thing you can do to move fast and
make winning offers.
