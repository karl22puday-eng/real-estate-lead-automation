# Crestview Realty — Areas We Serve

We actively work the metro core and the surrounding commuter belt. Below are the areas we
cover most, with the kind of buyer they tend to suit. If your target isn't listed, ask —
we likely still cover it or can refer a trusted partner.

## Downtown & Midtown
Condos, lofts, and townhomes for buyers who want walkability, nightlife, and a short
commute. Price points range from entry-level condos to premium high-rise units. Best for
first-time buyers, professionals, and downsizers.

## Westside & Garden District
Established, leafy family neighborhoods with single-family homes, good schools, and larger
lots. Popular with move-up buyers and families. Mid-to-upper price range.

## Northgate & the New-Construction Communities
Newer subdivisions with modern single-family homes and townhomes, amenities like pools and
parks, and builder incentives. Great for families wanting new construction and predictable
HOAs.

## Riverside & Old Town
Character homes, historic streets, and a mix of renovated and fixer-upper properties.
Suits buyers who want charm and are open to some renovation, plus value-add investors.

## Suburban Commuter Towns (Fairmont, Lake Hollow, Crestview Heights)
More space for the money — larger homes and lots a short drive from the core. Popular with
families prioritizing size, yards, and schools over a downtown commute.

## Investment focus areas
For buy-and-hold and value-add investors, Riverside, Old Town, and parts of Northgate
offer the best mix of rent demand and entry price. We'll run rent estimates and basic
return numbers on any specific property.

## Not sure which area fits?
Tell us your budget, must-haves, and how far you're willing to commute, and we'll point
you to the neighborhoods that actually match — and tell you honestly where your budget
will and won't stretch.
