# Crestview Realty — FAQ

**What does Crestview Realty do?**
We're a full-service residential brokerage. We help people buy, sell, rent, and invest in
homes across the metro area, with a dedicated agent for every client.

**How do I get started?**
Send a short inquiry with what you're trying to do (buy, sell, rent, or invest), the area
you're interested in, your budget, and your timeline. You'll get a fast, personal
response — usually the same day — and we'll suggest next steps.

**Does it cost me anything to work with a buyer's agent?**
In most transactions, the buyer's agent commission is paid through the deal at closing, so
buyer representation is typically free to you. We confirm the exact arrangement in writing
before you make an offer.

**What's your commission to sell my home?**
Listing commission is negotiable and agreed in writing before we list. We'll explain
exactly what's included — pricing analysis, prep advice, professional photography,
MLS + portal marketing, showings, negotiation, and transaction management.

**How much do I need to buy a home?**
It depends on the loan. Many conventional loans allow 3–5% down; 20% avoids PMI. Plan for
2–5% of the price in closing costs too. The best first step is getting pre-approved so you
know your real budget. (See the financing doc.)

**Do I need to be pre-approved before looking?**
For serious shopping, yes. Pre-approval sets your real budget and makes your offers
competitive — in many markets sellers won't consider an offer without it. We can refer
trusted lenders. Cash buyers should be ready to show proof of funds.

**How long does it take to buy or sell?**
Buying: once you're pre-approved and decisive, you can be under contract in days and
closed in about 30–45 days (faster for cash). Selling: well-priced homes often go under
contract within a couple of weeks, then ~30–45 days to close.

**What areas do you cover?**
The metro core and surrounding suburbs — family neighborhoods, new-construction
communities, downtown condos, and nearby commuter towns. Tell us your target area and
price range and we'll confirm quickly whether it's a market we actively work.

**What's my home worth?**
We'll prepare a real comparative market analysis (CMA) based on your home's condition,
updates, and recent comparable sales — far more accurate than an automated online
estimate. Send your address and a few details, no obligation.

**Can you help investors?**
Yes. We help with buy-and-hold and value-add residential properties, run the basic
numbers (rent estimate, cap rate, cash-on-cash), and can connect you with property
management.

**How fast will someone get back to me?**
Fast — responsiveness is the core of how we work. Most inquiries get a personal reply the
same day, and urgent buyers/sellers are prioritized.

**Do you help with rentals?**
Yes — we place qualified tenants and represent landlords on residential rentals,
including lease paperwork and move-in coordination.
