# Crestview Realty — Selling Your Home

We sell homes for the best price the market will support, in the timeline you need, with
as little disruption to your life as possible.

## Step 1 — Comparative Market Analysis (CMA)
We analyze recent comparable sales, current competition, and market trends in your
neighborhood to recommend a **listing price**. Pricing right from day one matters more
than anything — overpriced homes sit, go stale, and ultimately sell for *less*.

## Step 2 — Prep & stage
We advise on repairs, decluttering, and light staging that actually move the needle on
price. We arrange professional photography (and video/3D tour for higher-value homes)
because most buyers see your home online first.

## Step 3 — List & market
Your home goes live on the **MLS** and syndicates to the major portals (Zillow,
Realtor.com, Redfin, etc.). We add it to our buyer network, run social and email
promotion, and host showings and open houses.

## Step 4 — Offers & negotiation
We review every offer with you — not just price, but contingencies, financing strength,
and timeline — and negotiate to maximize your net proceeds and certainty of closing.

## Step 5 — Under contract → closing
We manage inspection, appraisal, and the buyer's financing through to closing, keeping
the deal on track. Typical time from listing to closing is a few weeks of marketing plus
~30–45 days under contract, but it varies with the market.

## What it costs to sell
- **Listing commission:** competitive and agreed in writing before we list. Commissions
  are negotiable; we'll explain exactly what's included and how any buyer-agent
  compensation is handled for your sale.
- **Seller closing costs:** typically include title/escrow fees and prorated taxes.
- **Optional prep:** staging or repairs, only where they pay for themselves.

## What's my home worth?
The honest answer needs a CMA — automated online "estimates" are often off by a wide
margin because they can't see your home's condition, updates, or street. Send us your
address and a few details and we'll prepare a real valuation, no obligation.

## How fast can I sell?
Well-priced, well-presented homes in active neighborhoods often go under contract within
a couple of weeks. Pricing and presentation are the two levers that control speed —
that's where we focus.
