# Crestview Realty — About & Services

Crestview Realty is a full-service residential real estate brokerage. We help people
**buy, sell, rent, and invest** in homes across the metro area, and we pair every client
with a dedicated agent who knows the neighborhood. We work by referral and repeat
business, so our whole model is built on responsiveness — most new inquiries get a
personal reply the same day.

## Who we help

### Buyers
First-time buyers, move-up buyers, and relocations. We handle the whole journey:
needs assessment, lender referral and pre-approval, home search and showings, offer
strategy, negotiation, inspection, and closing. Buyer representation is **free to the
buyer** — our commission is paid from the transaction at closing.

### Sellers
We price your home with a data-backed comparative market analysis (CMA), prep and stage
it, market it across the MLS and major portals, run showings and open houses, and
negotiate to close. Listing commission is competitive and discussed up front (see
financing & fees doc).

### Renters & landlords
We place qualified tenants and represent landlords on residential rentals, including
lease paperwork and move-in coordination.

### Investors
We help investors find buy-and-hold and value-add residential properties, run the basic
numbers (rent estimate, cap rate, cash-on-cash), and connect them with property managers.

## Areas we serve
We cover the metro core and surrounding suburbs — established family neighborhoods,
new-construction communities, downtown condos, and nearby commuter towns. If you tell us
the area and price range you're targeting, we'll let you know quickly whether it's a
market we actively work and pair you with the right agent. (See the **areas** doc for the
neighborhoods we cover most.)

## How working with us starts
1. You send a short inquiry (what you want to buy/sell/rent, your area, budget, timeline).
2. You get a fast, personal response and, if you're ready, a no-pressure consultation.
3. We map out next steps — for buyers that's usually pre-approval; for sellers it's a
   CMA and a walkthrough.

## What makes us different
- **Speed.** We respond fast because in real estate the first agent to reply usually wins
  the relationship — and buyers lose homes by waiting.
- **Honesty about fit.** If your timeline or budget isn't a match for the area you want,
  we'll tell you straight and help you adjust rather than waste your time.
- **One point of contact.** A dedicated agent, not a call-center handoff.
